The health of the Large Marine Ecosystems (LMEs) is represented through a series of indicators, grouped by categories: productivity, fish and fisheries, pollution, socio-economics and governance. In addition, some of those indicators are also available for the Western Pacific Warm Pool (WPWP). The LMEs are displayed in green on the above map, and the WPWP is displayed in blue.

You can display those indicators by selecting them in one of the categories in the right hand side menu. Click on a category to list the available indicator, then click on the indicator to display it. This view let you see a global view of an indicator.

To get more information about a specific LME, you can click on an LME on the map to select it, then click on the “read the LME factsheet” button that appears on top of the map. Alternatively, you can search for an LME by typing either its code number (from 1 to 66) or a part of its name in the search box. You can also click on the “Choose an LME” button that will display the complete list of LMEs.
